Application Instructions

Preparation
Draw the entire 3ml solution into a 3ml syringe for accurate dosing.
Injection Layer
Administer the product into the dermis layer to achieve optimal results, as
it is specifically formulated for this depth.
Method
For precision, use a mesotherapy gun set to a depth of 1.5mm. This allows
for even distribution and minimizes the risk of creating papules in the
epidermis.
Precautions
Avoid injecting into the epidermis to prevent surface papule formation.
Ensure aseptic techniques to reduce the risk of infection and irritation.
