## 활성성분 설명 / What is Copper peptide
Copper peptide is compounds that consist of small protein fragments bound to copper ions.
Copper peptide offer comprehensive benefits, enhancing wound healing with improved wound contraction, faster granular tissue development, and enhanced angiogenesis.
They also elevate antioxidant enzyme levels.
