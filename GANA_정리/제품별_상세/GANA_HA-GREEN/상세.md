# GANA HA - GREEN

## 제품명 / Product name
GANA HA - GREEN (GANA HA TYPE + Chlorophyllin)

## 분류 / Category
Dermal filler (cross-linked hyaluronic acid) — GANA RAINBOW series, GREEN variant

## 핵심 성분 & INCI / Core ingredients & INCI
| No. | INCI |
|---|---|
| | Water |
| 1 | Hyaluronic Acid |
| 2 | Chlorophyllin copper sodium salt |

## 활성성분 설명 / What is Chlorophyllin copper sodium salt
Chlorophyllin copper sodium salt is a naturally derived compound extracted from chlorophyll-containing vegetables.

## 특징 / Characteristics
- Shape: Green viscous gel form

## 스펙 / Specification
공통 GANA HA 베이스 스펙 적용 (Cross linking ratio 10%, HA concentration 20-24 mg/cc, BDDE, 12-18 months, 200-500 µm particle, 27G). 본 변형 전용 추가 스펙은 카탈로그에 별도 표기 없음.
