## 성분별 작용 / Key Ingredients and Actions

### Poly-L-Lactic Acid (PLLA) — 1 mg/ml
PLLA acts as a powerful collagen stimulator, initiating a natural regenerative process that gradually enhances skin elasticity, firmness, and smoothness by boosting collagen production. This ingredient is particularly effective for long-term improvement in skin density and structure.

### Hyaluronic Acid — 3 mg/ml
The cross-linked hyaluronic acid provides deep hydration, helping the skin retain moisture and maintain a plump, youthful appearance. Its high molecular weight ensures it reaches deep into the skin layers, supporting immediate hydration and a softening effect.

### Polydeoxyribonucleic Acid (PDRN) — 2 mg/ml
This DNA-based component is known for its regenerative properties, encouraging cellular repair and turnover. It revitalizes skin cells, aids in wound healing, and contributes to the overall texture and resilience of the skin by enhancing skin's repair mechanisms.

### Glutathione — 0.5 mg
Known as a potent antioxidant and skin brightener, glutathione plays a significant role in skin lightening by inhibiting melanin production. It provides a visible radiance and helps in reducing the appearance of age spots, pigmentation, and uneven skin tone.

## 적용법 / Application Instructions

### Preparation
Draw the entire 3ml solution into a 3ml syringe for accurate dosing.

### Injection Layer
Administer the product into the dermis layer to achieve optimal results, as it is specifically formulated for this depth.

### Method
For precision, use a mesotherapy gun set to a depth of 1.5mm. This allows for even distribution and minimizes the risk of creating papules in the epidermis.

### Precautions
- Avoid injecting into the epidermis to prevent surface papule formation.
- Ensure aseptic techniques to reduce the risk of infection and irritation.
