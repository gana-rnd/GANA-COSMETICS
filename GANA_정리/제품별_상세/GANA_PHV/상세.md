## 활성성분 설명 / What is PLLA
Poly(L-Lactide) is a biodegradable material with good bio-absorbability and bio-compatibility. After degradation in the body, PLLA products are excreted by metabolism and thus has no harmful side effects to the human body. It is for these reasons that PLLA products are currently widely used in the field of medicine, such as for one-time infusion equipment, medical surgical suture, drug delivery carriers, tissue engineering scaffolds, bone anchors, injectable microcapsules, microspheres, implants, elastomer materials for organ support, etc.

## 메커니즘 / Mechanism — PLLA degradation
PLLA (Poly-L-lactic acid) → Polymer of Lactic acid → Lactic acid → CO2 + H2O

Immune reaction pathway:
- PLLA → Immune reaction
- PLLA eaten by macrophage
- By immune reaction, activate fibroblast to produce collagen

PLLA degradation steps:
1. **Random enzymatic degradation** → Reduction in molecular weight
2. **Low molecular PLLA phagocyted by macrophage**

## 특징·장점 / The merits of GANA PHV
1. Early and longer volume augmentation over than 2 years by cross linked hyaluronic acid gel, and later volume augmentation by the collagen formation by PLLA.
2. The lower initial swelling by the absence of carboxymethylcellulose.
3. The immediate correction is possible by hyaluronidase.
4. Injected PLLA induce collagen synthesis; duration of GANA PHV is much longer than HA fillers.
5. Newly formed collagen prohibit the migration of GANA PHV.

## 볼륨 곡선 / GANA PHV Volume (timeline)

- Initial volume from Hyaluronic acid dermal fillers (HA), later sustained volume from PLLA + HA / collagen formation.
