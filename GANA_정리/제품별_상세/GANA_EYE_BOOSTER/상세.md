## 활성성분 설명 / What is PDRN
Polydeoxyribonucleotide is derived from sperm trout through an extraction process. The compounds are able to stimulate Vascular Endothelial Growth Factors and certain receptors in the body that trigger the creation of new artery formation, improves blood flow, and reduces inflammation. Most importantly, PDRN provides the nucleotides necessary for cell regeneration.

In addition to its use for diabetic foot ulcers, PDRN has proven effective in treating PAOD, chronic wounds, hind limb ischemia, osteoarthritis disease, coronary artery disease, and burn injuries.
