# GANA HA (GANA RAINBOW)

## 제품명 / Product name
GANA HA — GANA RAINBOW (Rainbow filler line overview)

## 분류 / Category
Dermal filler (cross-linked hyaluronic acid) — Rainbow series base product

## 소개 / Overview
Experience the Natural Radiance of GANA RAINBOW Cosmetics – Crafted with GANA HA and Pure Ingredients.

The GANA RAINBOW line is built on the GANA HA cross-linked hyaluronic acid base, available in four color variants (RED, YELLOW, BLUE, GREEN) each combined with a different active ingredient.

## 핵심 성분 & INCI / Core ingredients & INCI
- Water
- Hyaluronic Acid

## 특징·장점 / Merits
1. **High cross linking ratio** — It has a 10% cross linking ratio, which is higher than other products with 6-8% monophasic and 2-4% biophasic.
2. **High HA concentration** — It has a high HA concentration of 20-24 mg/cc.
3. **Small particle size** — It has small particle size so that doctors are easy to mold shape.
4. **Low extruding force** — It ensures a smoother, more precise application, enhancing its effectiveness and providing superior, natural-looking results.
5. **Long duration** — It has a maintenance period of 12-18 months.
