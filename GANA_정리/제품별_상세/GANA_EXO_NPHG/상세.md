## 활성성분 설명 / Active ingredients

### What is Extracellular vesicles (EVs)?
Extracellular vesicles (EVs) are small lipid membrane-enclosed structures secreted by cells, and among them, EVs with a size of approximately 40 to 100 nm are called exosomes.

### What is Centella?
The leaves and stems of the plant contain ingredients such as madecassic acid, asiatic acid, and asiaticoside that help strengthen the skin barrier, protect the skin, and soothe irritation. It also has the power to relieve inflammation and heal wounds such as tumors and ulcers, and as it has been discovered, it is widely used as a raw material for ointments, toothpaste, and cosmetics.

### What is Nicotinamide mononucleotide (NMN)?
1. **Anti-aging properties**: NMN can help reduce various age-related changes in the skin by increasing intracellular NAD+ levels, which stimulates energy production and activates sirtuin genes.
2. **Improved skin hydration**: Studies have shown that NMN application activates the sirtuin gene and promotes the production of natural moisturizing factors, which may help prevent skin aging.
3. **Reduced melanin production**: In aged melanocytes, NMN treatment showed a marked reduction in melanin production. This effect was also observed in a reconstituted human skin model with aged melanocytes, suggesting potential benefits for aging-related hyperpigmentation.
4. **Metabolic improvements**: Since NAD+ is involved in the metabolism of many cells, applying NMN may help improve skin problems associated with disturbed metabolism.
5. **Better absorption**: Cosmetics containing NMN allow for more direct absorption into the skin compared to oral supplements or food sources. Research has shown that when NMN serum is applied to the stratum corneum, the amount of NMN in cells increases approximately 100-fold, while NAD+ levels increase about 4-fold.
6. **Potential for improving various skin issues**: NMN's ability to activate sirtuin genes and increase NAD+ levels may help address multiple skin concerns simultaneously.
